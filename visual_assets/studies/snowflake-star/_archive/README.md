# Archive — superseded snowflake-star explorations

Left-behind iterations, kept for the record. The **active** design lives one level
up: the **12-point star locked to the leaf's six rays** (`../star12-*`, `../_gen5.py`,
`../board5.svg`, `../star12-proof.*`).

- **Iteration 1 — dendrite/snowflake** (`_gen.py`, `_board.py`, `board.svg*`,
  `star10-*`, `star11-*`, `star13-dendrite-*`, `star13-cells`, `star13-compass-seal`):
  frost-fern arms. Rejected — read as a cluttered **sun**.
- **Iteration 2 — compass-star, alternating points** (`_gen2.py`, `_board2.py`,
  `board2*.svg*`, `c10-*`, `c11-*`, `c13-*`): alternating long/short fixed the sun
  read. Superseded once the points were locked to the leaf rays.
- **Iteration 3 — leaf→star morph** (`_gen3.py`, `morph-*`, `skel-star6-*`,
  `board3.svg*`): proved the leaf IS a clipped 6-ray burst; produced the 6-point
  "released leaf" star. The 6-point remains the alternative to the 12-point.
- **Iteration 4 — first 12-point colour pass** (`_gen4.py`, `star12-cells*`,
  `star12-obs*`, `star12-seal*`, `board4.svg*`): the 12-point with a central hub.
  Superseded by iteration 5 (hub removed, leaf-faithful colour map + outline family).
