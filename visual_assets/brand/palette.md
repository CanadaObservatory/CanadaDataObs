# Canada Observatory palette — locked 2026-06-10

Eight colours plus two utility tints. WCAG-audited 2026-06-10 (ratios on
Warm Off-White unless noted).

| Colour | Hex | Role | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Deep Navy | `#17324D` | navbar, headings, dark surfaces | 12.0:1 on off-white |
| Lake Blue | `#2A7F9E` | large accents, UI elements | 4.1:1 — fails AA for body text on off-white; use the link tint below |
| Deep Maroon | `#7A263A` | the brand mark, emphasis | 8.8:1; distinct from chart Canada-red `#d62728` (data ink only) |
| Boreal Green | `#3F6F5E` | nature accent; Land & Environment | 5.3:1 |
| Prairie Gold | `#C2972F` | warm accent; Economy & Affordability | 2.5:1 — **never text/thin strokes on light**; pair with navy ink (4.9:1); best on navy |
| Warm Off-White | `#F7F4EE` | page background, reversed text | 12.0:1 as text on navy |
| Slate Grey | `#6B7280` | secondary text | 4.4:1 on off-white (borderline) — prefer on white (4.8:1) or use navy |
| Border Grey | `#D9DEE5` | rules, dividers, borders | decorative only |

**Utility tints**

| Tint | Hex | Use |
|---|---|---|
| Link blue | `#1F6E8C` | hyperlinks / small interactive text on light (5.2:1, passes AA) |
| Wheat | `#D9B36C` | soft gold fills; the premium leaf colour on navy (6.6:1) |
| Deep Ochre | `#A8842F` | gold icons/borders on light where ≥3:1 is required (3.2:1) |

**Six-area accent mapping:** People & Society = maroon · Economy & Affordability =
prairie gold · Government & Public Finances = navy · Health & Well-being = lake ·
Land & Environment = boreal · Education, Science & Innovation = slate.

The maroon/navy pair is near-equiluminant (1.35:1): any mark using both must also
work as a one-colour silhouette (grayscale, print, reversed).
